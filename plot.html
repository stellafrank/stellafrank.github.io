What is Grey's Anatomy about?!

Grey's Anatomy is a television show focused around a group of doctors in Seattle. The show focuses on their careers, as well as their personal lives.

What makes this show standout from other medical TV shows?

It has many different elements than a typical medical show.

  • It has followed some of the same doctors for more than 14 years
  • It shows the realities of their lives as doctors and people
  • It is realistic (most of the time)
  • It has elements of drama and comedy, making it reflective of life )
  • It is not repetitive

What are some main events that have happened throughout the show?

Throughout the seasons, big events and disasters have affected the lives of the characters.

Here are some examples:

  • Many of the starting cast members died from different events
  • The doctors on the show were involved in a plane crash
  • There was a shooting that took place in the hospital
  • The hopsital was bought and renamed Grey-Sloan Memorial
