Grey's Anatomy Cast

The cast has changed over time, but the original cast followed a group of interns at the hospital. The show centers around Meredith Grey.

Some of the Cast:

  • Meredith Grey played by Ellen Pompeo. Meredith Grey started in season 1 as an intern and is currently the Chief of General Surgery. She was married to Derek Shepherd and has 3 kids.
  • Derek Shepherd played by Patrick Dempsey. In season 1, he was the Chief of Neurosurgery. He married Meredith Grey and had kids with her, prior to his death in season 11.
  • Christina Yang played by Sandra Oh. Christina Yang was an intern in the start of season 1 and eventually became a cardiothoracic fellow before moving away. She was best friends with Meredith Grey and was married to Owen Hunt, another doctor.
  • Alex Karev played by Justin Chambers. Alex Karev started as an intern. He eventually went on to become Chief of Surgery and Chief of Pediatric Surgery. He was married to Jo Wilson, another surgeon before divorcing her and leaving the show in season 16
  • Miranda Bailey played by Chandra Wilson. Miranda Bailey was in charge of the interns in season 1, but later became a general surgeon and Chief of Surgery. She has a son and marries Ben Warren, who also worked at the hospital.
  • Richard Webber played by James Pickens Jr. In season 1, Richard Webber was the Cheif of Surgery, but retired from that job and is currently the Director of the Residency Program and a general surgeon. He had a relationship with Meredith's mother who died, making him like a father to Meredith
